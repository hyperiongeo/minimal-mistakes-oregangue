### Dear Friends of the University of Saskatchewan Ore Gangue Geological Society: 

The Ore Gangue Geological Students' Society is the University of Saskatchewan's longest running student society. Founded in 1934, the society to this day continues to foster interest in the Earth Sciences, to help establish lifelong professional networks, and to inspire geoscientists and geological engineers alike. 

The Ore Gangue Alumni is hosting the annual Mashers Golf Tournament. The tournament will be hosted at the Elbow Springs Golf Course (240086 Lott Creek Dr, Calgary, AB T3Z 2V4). The golf tournament’s success depends on the gift of your sponsorship. 

You can support the Ore Gangue Golf Tournament through monetary or in-kind donations (Prizes or auction items). As the alumni are part of a non-profit group, the proceeds of your donation initially go towards the tournament and then towards sustaining the current society initiatives including the Michael D. Welch Memorial Book Prize and the U of S Ore Gangue Alumni Fund which has contributed over $41,000 over the years. Your contribution will help the organization thrive for years to come.
